About


A Selangorian by birth and getting an education in various states. My love affair with the written word began in the cozy corners of our local library, where, at the tender age of five, I took my first steps into the enchanting world of reading through a dramatization of a cherished picture book. Since that moment, the allure of the written narrative has been an irresistible force.

Classic literature and the cinematic magic of storytelling have always been my steadfast companions. Yet, I must confess to harboring an unspoken affection for the exquisite design and allure of the bookshelf—a passion that goes beyond the pages to appreciate the artistry behind each cover and the tactile pleasure of a well-bound volume.

In the vast landscape of information, my aim is to curate experiences that inspire readers to break free from the confines of conventional thinking. Whether it's weaving a narrative that challenges societal norms or fostering an environment that encourages exploration of new perspectives—be it social, racial, economic, or environmental. I am committed to fostering a library space that imagines and shapes a better future.

Just as the theatre has the power to transform minds, so does the library. I aspire to create a literary stage where the stories we tell can transcend boundaries, spark conversations, and, most importantly, inspire a community to envision and embrace positive change.
